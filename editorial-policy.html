Editorial Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

GoodVibe Daily publishes two core formats: short quote content and long-form editorial articles. This policy explains how we choose content, how we review quality, and how we handle updates. Our goal is simple: publish pages that are genuinely helpful to readers, not pages designed only to rank for keywords. We believe inspiration content can still be practical, accurate, and responsible.

1) Editorial mission and audience

Our content is created for readers looking for motivation, calm, healing, and sustainable productivity. We write for real-life use, not abstract inspiration. Each article should leave readers with at least one clear takeaway they can apply the same day: a reflection prompt, a habit, a decision framework, or a practical routine.

2) How we select quote content

Quotes are selected for clarity, emotional relevance, and usefulness. We prioritize language that helps readers think and act with intention. We avoid sensational, harmful, hateful, explicit, or manipulative material. When quote attributions are uncertain, we either verify with reliable references or avoid making a definitive attribution claim.

3) How we produce long-form articles

Every article is developed around a specific reader problem, such as improving morning focus, managing stress, or avoiding burnout. We aim for plain-language writing with practical structure: clear headings, examples, and concise steps. Articles should not be padded for length; they should be complete, readable, and relevant.

4) Quality standards

Before publishing, we check each page against these standards:

  • Originality: no copied sections, no duplicate blocks across multiple posts.
  • Usefulness: at least one concrete action, prompt, or framework for readers.
  • Readability: scannable headings, logical flow, and clear language.
  • Trust: accurate metadata, clear internal linking, and transparent intent.

5) Updates and corrections

We review and improve older content as the site grows. Updates may include clarifying guidance, improving structure, correcting errors, and adding better internal links. When meaningful edits are made, we refresh visible update dates and metadata where appropriate. If a material error is discovered, we correct it as quickly as possible.

6) Advertising and editorial independence

GoodVibe may display advertising, including AdSense placements. Ads do not determine editorial conclusions or article recommendations. We do not publish low-value pages solely to increase ad impressions. Content quality and reader experience are prioritized over ad density, especially above the fold.

7) AI use and human review

Drafting tools may assist with formatting or brainstorming, but final content is reviewed and edited to ensure quality, clarity, and usefulness. We do not intentionally publish low-value automated text. Every published article must pass a human editorial check for tone, utility, and consistency with this policy.

8) Reader feedback

Reader feedback helps us identify gaps and improve quality. If you spot a concern or want to suggest a topic, please use our Contact page. We review feedback regularly and use it to prioritize updates and new editorial content.
